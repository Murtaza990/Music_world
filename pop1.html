Jubin Nautiyal

Personal Details

  • Full Name: Jubin Nautiyal
  • Date of Birth: October 14, 1989
  • Birthplace: Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India
  • Nationality: Indian
  • Occupation: Playback Singer
  • Years Active: 2014–present
  • Spouse: Not Married
  • Children: None

Early Life

Jubin Nautiyal was born in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. His interest in music started at a young age, and he received formal training in music.

Career Beginnings

Jubin Nautiyal gained recognition with his debut song "Ek Mulaqat" from the movie Sonali Cable (2014). The song received positive reviews and marked his entry into Bollywood.

Career Achievements

  • Mirchi Music Awards: Listeners’ Choice Award for Best Male Vocalist of the Year for "Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata" (2016)
  • Zee Cine Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Soch Na Sake" (2016)
  • Screen Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Bawara Mann" (2018)
  • IIFA Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Bawara Mann" (2018)

Notable Songs

  • "Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata" - Bajrangi Bhaijaan
  • "Soch Na Sake" - Airlift
  • "Bawara Mann" - Jolly LLB 2
  • "Tum Hi Aana" - Marjaavaan
  • "Humnava Mere" - Jubin Nautiyal Version

Personal Interests

Jubin Nautiyal enjoys exploring different genres of music and collaborating with other musicians. He is also passionate about fitness and outdoor activities.

Recent Activities

Jubin Nautiyal continues to be a prominent figure in the music industry, delivering soulful performances and gaining popularity among music enthusiasts.
