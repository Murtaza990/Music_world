Arijit Singh

Personal Details

  • Full Name: Arijit Singh
  • Date of Birth: April 25, 1987
  • Birthplace: Jiaganj, Murshidabad, West Bengal, India
  • Nationality: Indian
  • Occupation: Playback Singer, Music Composer
  • Years Active: 2005–present
  • Spouse: Koel Roy (m. 2014)
  • Children: Two sons

Early Life

Arijit Singh was born to a Punjabi father and a Bengali mother. His early training in music was under the guidance of Rajendra Prasad Hazari, who taught him Indian classical music. He also received training in tabla from Dhirendra Prasad Hazari and Rabindra Sangeet and pop music from Birendra Prasad Hazari.

Career Achievements

  • Filmfare Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Tum Hi Ho" from "Aashiqui 2" (2013), "Sooraj Dooba Hain" from "Roy" (2016), "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" from "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" (2017)
  • IIFA Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Tum Hi Ho" (2014), "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" (2017)
  • Zee Cine Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Tum Hi Ho" (2014), "Sooraj Dooba Hain" (2016), "Channa Mereya" from "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" (2017)
  • Stardust Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Tum Hi Ho" (2014), "Muskurane" from "CityLights" (2015)
  • Screen Awards: Best Male Playback Singer for "Tum Hi Ho" (2014), "Soch Na Sake" from "Airlift" (2016), "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" (2017)
  • Mirchi Music Awards: Listeners’ Choice Award for Best Male Vocalist of the Year for multiple songs
