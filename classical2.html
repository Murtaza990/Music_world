Early Life

Mustafa Zahid was born in Lahore, Pakistan, and developed an interest in music at a young age. He started his music career as the lead vocalist of the Pakistani band Roxen.

Career Highlights

Mustafa Zahid gained fame with his band's debut album "Rozen-e-Deewar" in 2006. He later ventured into Bollywood, providing hit songs for several movies, and became known for his soulful voice and rock-infused style.

Career Achievements

  • Lead vocalist of the band Roxen, which released the successful album "Rozen-e-Deewar".
  • Bollywood hits include "Toh Phir Aao" and "Tera Mera Rishta" from the movie "Awarapan".
  • Collaborated with numerous artists and contributed to the soundtracks of several Bollywood films.
  • Known for blending rock music with traditional Pakistani music elements.

Notable Songs

  • "Toh Phir Aao" - Awarapan
  • "Tera Mera Rishta" - Awarapan
  • "Bhula Dena" - Aashiqui 2
  • "Hum Jee Lenge" - Murder 3
  • "Zaroorat" - Ek Villain

Personal Interests

Mustafa Zahid has a deep passion for music and enjoys exploring different genres. He is also involved in various charitable activities and supports music education initiatives.

Recent Activities

Mustafa Zahid continues to produce music and perform live. He remains an influential figure in the Pakistani music scene and regularly collaborates with other artists.
